Went on a 10 day trip to Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh, Visited Taj-Mahal for the first time! Our Itinerary
  • Day 1 and 2, Udaipur: The City Palace, Lake Pichhola, Saheliyon Ki Badi, and ate mostly street food.
  • Day 3 and 4, Jaipur: City Palace, Amber Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Dal Bati at Virasat Restaurant
  • Day 5 Mehandipur: Visited the Sacred Balaji Temple, Stayed in a dharam-shala(pilgrim lodging).
  • Day 6 and 7, Vrindavan and Mathura: Isckon Temple, Banke Bihari Temple, Prem Mandir, Janmabhoomi, had a lot of sweet rose lassi and aloo tikkis!
  • Day 8 and 9 Agra: The Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, Gurudwara Guru Ka Taal, and meals at Good Vibes Cafe
