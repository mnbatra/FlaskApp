Cypher 2017, an analytics and data science summit, held in Bangalore. The summit touched all aspects of data science, data analytics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and also had workshops for professionals to get a handle on the latest technologies. A great many speakers from the companies: Deloitte, Pepsico, IBM, Walmart, Yatra, Wipro, American Express, and Many others, took part in the Summit Sessions that interested me:
  • Sandeep Mittal, Director of Cartesian Consulting - Storytelling using Data
  • Anand S, CEO of Gramener - Analytics Automation
  • Ankur Sharma, Head of Analytics at Instamojo - Analytics at an early-stage company
